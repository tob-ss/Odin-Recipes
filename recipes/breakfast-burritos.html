Breakfast Burritos

Description

Whenever I'm trying to bulk I found it's often hard to stay consistent eating in the morning. Whether it's due to the morning commute or morning classes, I never have enough time to make a decent breakfast.

These breakfast burritos helped me solve this issue.

They're easy to make and even easier to prepare in the morning!

Ingredients

Burrito Seasoning Mix

  • 1 tbsp Brown Sugar
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Pepper
  • 1 tsp Cayenne Pepper
  • 1 tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 1 tsp Onion Powder
  • 1 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 tsp Oregano
  • 1.5 tsp Rubbed Sage
  • 1 tbsp Chicken Stock

Ingredients

  • Burrito Seasoning Mix
  • 1 tbsp Oil
  • 450g Ground Turkey
  • 8 Large Eggs
  • 2 tbsp Milk
  • 1 Green Pepper
  • 1 Red Pepper
  • 1 Onion
  • 140g Spinach
  • 1 Potato
  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • 120g Shredded Cheese
  • 5-8 Large Tortillas

Steps

  1. Add turkey and burrito seasoning mix to a large bowl. Mix thoroughly.
  2. In a large skillet over a medium-high heat, add 1tbsp of oil and add in the turkey.
  3. Cook turkey until completely browned with no pink colour and and set aside until later.
  4. Wash vegetables, cut peppers, potato and onions into a dice.
  5. Cut spinach into smaller pieces.
  6. Whisk 8 eggs with milk and turkey seasoning mix in seperate bowl
  7. In a large skillet over a medium-high heat, add 1 tbsp of oil and add in peppers, chopped potatoes and onions (season with a pinch of salt and add in seasoning mix).
  8. Once the onions are translucent and potatoes are softer, add in spinach, stir to combine.
  9. Once spinach has wilted, add turkey back into the pot, stir and cook egg mixture in seperate pan
  10. Continuously stir egg into center until egg begins to stick in center
  11. Flip egg into an omelette and add to turkey mix
  12. Mix and taste. Adjust flavour with salt and pepper if needed.
  13. Assemble burritos, adding the shredded cheese on top of each burritos before rolling.
  14. Fill as many torillas as possible then freeze burritos.
  15. When you're ready to eat a burrito, place in microwave to defrost for 10-20mins, then place in oven for 5-10 mins.
  16. Enjoy :)
