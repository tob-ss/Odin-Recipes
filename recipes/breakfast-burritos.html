Breakfast Burritos

Description

Whenever I'm trying to bulk I found it's often hard to stay consistent eating in the morning. Whether it's due to the morning commute or morning classes, I never have enough time to make a decent breakfast.

These breakfast burritos helped me solve this issue.

They're easy to make and even easier to prepare in the morning!

Ingredients

Turkey Filling

  • 450g Ground Turkey
  • 1 tbsp Brown Sugar
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Pepper
  • 1 tsp Cayenne Pepper
  • 1 tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 1 tsp Onion Powder
  • 1 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 tsp Oregano
  • 1.5 tsp Rubbed Sage
  • 1 tbsp Oil

Burrito Filling

  • 8 Large Eggs
  • 2 tbsp Milk
  • 1 Green Pepper
  • 1 Red Pepper
  • 1 Onion
  • 140g Spinach
  • 170g Shredded Potato
  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • 120g Shredded Cheese
  • 5-8 Large Tortillas

Steps

  1. Add turkey, turkey seasonings and brown sugar to a large bowl. Mix thoroughly.
  2. In a large skillet over a medium-high heat, add 1tbsp of oil and add in the turkey.
  3. Cook turkey until completely browned with no pink colour, and set aside until later.
  4. Wash vetables and cut peppers and onions into a dice. Cut spinach into smaller spieces.
  5. With a grater, grate potatoes into shredded pieces.
  6. In a large skillet over a medium-high heat, add 1 tbsp of oil and add in peppers and onions (season with a pinch of salt).
  7. Once the onions are translucent, add in shredded potatoes and spinach, stir to combine.
  8. Once spinach has wilted, create room in the center and crack 8 eggs in the center.
  9. Cook the eggs in the center for a few minutes until they are cooked.
  10. Once cooked, mix and taste. Adjust flavour with salt and pepper if needed.
  11. Allow mixture to cool, then assemble burritos, adding the shredded cheese on top of each burritos before rolling.
  12. Fill as many torillas as possible then freeze burritos.
  13. When you're ready to eat a burrito, place in microwave to defrost for 10-20mins, then place in oven for 5-10 mins.
  14. Enjoy :)
