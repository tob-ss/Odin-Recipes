Bulking Shake

Description

So you want a protein shake that is high in protein, affordable and tastes good? Look no further!

This bulking shake is so good, you'll think you're drinking a Frankie and Benny's milkshake.

Ingredients

  • 300ml Whole Milk
  • 1 Banana
  • 30g Peanut Butter
  • 25g Vanilla or Chocolate Whey Protein
  • 2 Kinder Bueno Bars (1 pack)

Steps

  1. Pour 300ml whole milk into a blender.
  2. Cut 1 banana into pieces, put in blender.
  3. Put 30g peanut butter into blender.
  4. Put 25g whey protein of choice into blender.
  5. Break up kinder bueno bars and put in blender.
  6. Blend all ingredients, pour into your cup of choice and enjoy :)
